Cheese Pasta

  • Pasta (200g)
  • Butter (2 tbsp)
  • All-purpose flour (2 tbsp)
  • Milk (1.5 cups)
  • Cheddar cheese, shredded (1 cup)
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded (0.5 cup)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (optional, 2 tbsp)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Nutmeg (optional, a pinch)
